### For a mix of reasons I‚Äôd been anxious about birth

## Work‚Ä¶ or lack of!

As I mentioned above, I left work earlier than originally planned because of my health suffering in pregnancy, but I‚Äôm proud of how I got everything set up well for my absence. Running the process to appoint my interim cover left me with lots of confidence that the team would be in a good place.

## Lots of much-needed home improvements
Since moving in years ago we‚Äôd done extremely little to the house, and in all honesty we‚Äôd been putting it off as we knew there was a lot of work. One of the biggest pieces was that we‚Äôd had cracking and water starting to come in through the bricks above windows whenever it rained badly.
